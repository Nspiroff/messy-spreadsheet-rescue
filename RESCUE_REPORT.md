## Repairs, itemized

- **5,100 dates normalized** from 5+ formats
  (879 were raw Excel serial numbers like `46045`)
- **5,150 prices parsed** out of currency text
- **27 decimal-shift typos fixed** (a $42 item entered as $4,200)
- **664 customer name variants merged** (704 raw spellings → 40 real customers)
- **1,808 category labels repaired** (case, typos, aliases)
- **3,174 regions standardized** ("Virginia", "va " → VA)
- **369 order IDs relabeled** (`ord 12345` → `ORD-12345`)
- **52 fake-missing cells standardized** ("N/A", "NULL", "-" → truly blank)
- **2,697 cells de-whitespaced**

## Quarantined rows (never silently guessed)

Anything ambiguous goes to `data/quarantine.csv` with a reason, so a
human makes the call — not the script:

- 50 × unparseable or missing date
- 33 × negative quantity (possible return) - needs human review
- 15 × unparseable quantity
